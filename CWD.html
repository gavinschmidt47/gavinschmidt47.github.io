Corporate: World Dominion

MODIFIED BOARD GAME

"Corporate: World Dominion" is a thrilling reimagining of the classic board game, Risk, where corporate conquest is all that remains. Your mission? Expand your business empire as far as the eye can see, outmaneuvering and eliminating local and global competitors by any means necessary.

Players start with their factories located across different regions of the world. Utilize the economy system to strengthen your forces, seize more territories, and establish complete control over the world's market.

WHAT I DID

The entire design and mechanical function of the game were done by me. We were assigned to reimagine a classic board game within 2 weeks. I chose a personal favorite and iconic board game, Risk. I also decided that I would like to add an economy system in some way. After extensive brainstorming, I found a way to enhance the game through an economy and building system.
