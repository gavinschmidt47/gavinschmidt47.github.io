Super Bionic Bash Minigame: Bysen Says

GROUP ORIGINAL MINIGAME GAMEJAM

Limbitless Solutions hosted a game jam where teams created original minigames. Our team, consisting of talented individuals, came together to develop "Bysen Says", a unique twist on the Mario Party minigame "Time to Shine." This game jam consisted of members of the Limbitless team as well as students from the University of Skövde, Sweden.

WHAT I DID

I created the music and helped lineup the sound with the programming events in the game. I also contributed to the game design and overall concept, helping to create the scoring system. I made the HUD elements to ensure an exciting gameplay experience, as well.
