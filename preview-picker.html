COLDSNAP Preview Picker

Local tool — not deployed to the site

How to use: Pick a 20–45 second clip from each COLDSNAP track. These will be the embedded previews on the music page and COLDSNAP project page.
  1. Press Play on a track and listen.
  2. When you hear where you want the preview to start, click Set Start.
  3. When you hear where you want it to end, click Set End.
  4. You can also type times directly into the number fields (in seconds).
  5. Click Preview Range to hear exactly your selected start-to-end clip. Click again to stop early.
  6. When all four are set, click Export Config, copy the output, and paste it back to Copilot.
